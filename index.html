Our Mission

The Challenge:

Sunapee Harbor is alive with creative energy ‚Äî multiple venues, passionate organizers, talented performers. But without coordination, we're competing instead of collaborating. Events clash. Audiences get split. The community's voice gets scattered.

The Solution:

The Sunapee Sound Project exists to bring everyone together ‚Äî to create a shared calendar, coordinate efforts, and build a permanent performance space that serves the entire community. When we work together, every event succeeds. Every voice is heard. Every Friday night matters.

Core Vision:

A permanent lakeside performance space that doubles as a philanthropic engine ‚Äî where coordinated events generate community reinvestment through music, culinary art, and local creativity. One calendar. One community. Unlimited possibilities.

Why It Matters

Sunapee Harbor already has everything it needs: talented performers, dedicated venue owners, passionate organizers, and a community that loves to gather. What we've been missing is coordination.

When events compete for the same night, everyone loses. When we work together ‚Äî sharing calendars, promoting each other, building common infrastructure ‚Äî everyone thrives.

The Sunapee Sound Project is the glue that binds us. A shared stage. A common purpose. A united voice. This is how a scattered scene becomes a movement.
